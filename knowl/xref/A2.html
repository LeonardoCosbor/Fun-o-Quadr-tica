Item 2.
Seja a função quadrática \(f(x)=-\dfrac{1}{2}(x-2)^2-3\text{.}\) Analise os valores de y para \(x\in\{-6,-4,-2,0,2\}\text{,}\) mostrando quais são as coordenadas do vértice e quais são os valores de \(x\) possuem os mesmos valores de \(y\text{.}\)
Solução.
\begin{equation*} f(-6)=-\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot(-6+2)^2-3=-\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot(-4)^2-3=-\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot16-3=-8-3=-11 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} f(-4)=-\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot(-4+2)^2-3=-\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot(-2)^2-3=-\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot4-3=-2-3=-5 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} f(-2)=-\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot(-2+2)^2-3=-\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot(0)^2-3=-\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot0-3=-3 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} f(0)=-\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot(0+2)^2-3=-\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot(2)^2-3=-\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot4-3=-2-3=-5 \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} f(2)=-\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot(2+2)^2-3=-\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot(4)^2-3=-\dfrac{1}{2}\cdot16-3=-8-3=-11 \end{equation*}
Note que os valores de y começam a repetir a partir do ponto \((-2,-3)\text{.}\) Isto significa que Este ponto é o vértice da parábola. Logo,
\begin{equation*} f(-2)=-3\text{,} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} f(-4)=f(0)=-5 \end{equation*}
e
\begin{equation*} f(-6)=f(2)=-11\text{.} \end{equation*}
